== About LearnTrac ==

LearnTrac combines the power of AI with your learning materials to provide:

 * **Intelligent PDF Processing**: Upload textbooks and documents for AI-powered analysis
 * **Smart Q&A**: Ask questions about your uploaded content and get instant answers
 * **Personalized Learning**: Get recommendations based on your learning patterns
 * **Progress Tracking**: Monitor your understanding and growth over time

== Getting Started ==

1. **Login**: Click the login link in the navigation bar to access LearnTrac features
2. **Upload Content**: Use the interface above to upload PDF textbooks
3. **Start Learning**: Ask questions and explore your content with AI assistance
